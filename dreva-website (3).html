Senior Brand Designer
Full-Time Remote OK London / Global Brand Identity
£55–75k
About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Brand Designer who can lead identity projects from discovery to delivery — someone who thinks in systems, sweats the details, and understands that brand is strategy made visible. You'll own end-to-end brand identity projects for our clients across fintech, SaaS, and consumer goods.

What You'll Do
  • Lead brand identity projects from brief to final delivery, including logo systems, typography, colour, and visual language
  • Create comprehensive brand guidelines and design tokens for client teams
  • Collaborate directly with strategy and UX teams to ensure brand coherence across all touchpoints
  • Present and articulate creative decisions to senior client stakeholders
  • Mentor junior designers and review work across the team
What We're Looking For
  • 5+ years of brand identity design experience at an agency or in-house
  • A portfolio demonstrating complete brand system work — not just logos
  • Mastery of Figma and Adobe Creative Suite
  • Strong understanding of typography, grid systems, and print production
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for client-facing work
Nice to Have
  • Motion design / After Effects experience
  • Experience with packaging or environmental design
  • Background in brand strategy or naming

Lead UX / Product Designer
Full-Time Remote UX Design
£65–85k
About the Role

As Lead UX/Product Designer, you'll shape the digital product experiences for some of the most interesting startups and scale-ups we work with. You'll combine user research, systems thinking, and high-fidelity execution to design products that are genuinely intuitive, conversion-optimised, and beautiful.

What You'll Do
  • Lead the full UX design process: research, information architecture, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity UI
  • Run user interviews and usability testing sessions, synthesising findings into actionable design decisions
  • Build scalable component libraries and design systems in Figma
  • Work hand-in-hand with developers to ensure pixel-perfect delivery
  • Champion accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) across all product design work
What We're Looking For
  • 6+ years of product/UX design experience, ideally at a fast-growth startup or top agency
  • Portfolio with shipped product work and measurable outcomes (conversion, retention, etc.)
  • Deep Figma expertise — variables, auto layout, component architecture
  • Strong grasp of interaction design, micro-animations, and state management
  • Ability to translate complex user needs into elegant, simple interfaces
🌍 Fully Remote 🏖️ Unlimited PTO 🎨 Design tool budget 📈 Equity options
